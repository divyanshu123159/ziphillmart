About ZiphillMart

A mountain-to-table marketplace built in the heart of Jakhnidhar.

Who We Are

Rooted in the
Himalayas

ZiphillMart was built from one simple belief: the farmers of Tehri Garhwal deserve a fair, direct market for their chemical-free produce. Nestled in the ridges of Jakhnidhar, our partner farmers have cultivated these lands for generations.

We connect farmers directly to buyers via WhatsApp — no warehouses, no markups, no middlemen. Every purchase directly supports a farming family.
