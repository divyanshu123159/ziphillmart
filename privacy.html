ZiphillMart ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using ZiphillMart, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

From Buyers:

  • No personal data is collected from buyers browsing the marketplace.
  • WhatsApp order links redirect to WhatsApp's platform. We do not store those conversations.

From Sellers (during Registration):

  • Full name and mobile number — used for account identity.
  • Product details: category, name, and price per kilogram.
  • Product photograph name reference (file not uploaded to our servers).
  • ID card and farm proof documents — for verification only, reviewed by admin Divyanshu.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To create and manage your seller account.
  • To display your products on the ZiphillMart marketplace.
  • To verify your identity and farm credentials before approval.
  • To contact you via WhatsApp about your account or listings.
  • To improve the ZiphillMart platform.

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or marketers.

3. Google Sheets & Data Storage

ZiphillMart uses Google Apps Script as a backend to store seller registrations in a Google Sheet. This means:

  • Seller registration data (name, mobile, category, product, price) is written to a private Google Sheet accessible only by admin Divyanshu.
  • Data is stored on Google's servers subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • The Google Sheet is not publicly accessible.
4. WhatsApp & Third Parties
  • Buy buttons redirect to WhatsApp, governed by WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • ZiphillMart does not access, intercept, or store WhatsApp conversations.
  • All transactions happen directly between buyer and farmer. ZiphillMart is not a party.
5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Request corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your seller account and data.

Contact us via WhatsApp: +91 79834 47349

6. Cookies

ZiphillMart does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics services. We use sessionStorage in your browser only to store your seller name for the dashboard greeting during your active session. This data is not transmitted to any server.
